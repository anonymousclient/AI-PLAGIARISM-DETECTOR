Help Center

Learn how to use the AI Plagiarism Checker system.

For Students
  1. Click "Login as Student" on the homepage
  2. Register with your name, email, and password (if new user)
  3. Verify your email with the OTP sent to your inbox
  4. Login and view the Student Dashboard
  5. Find the assignment and click "Upload Assignment"
  6. Select a PDF or DOCX file and submit
  7. You'll see a success message once uploaded
Note: Students cannot view plagiarism results. Only faculty can see similarity reports.
For Faculty
  1. Click "Login as Faculty" on the homepage
  2. Register and verify your email (if new user)
  3. Go to your Faculty Dashboard
  4. Click "Create Assignment" — enter subject and title
  5. Students will see the assignment on their dashboard
  6. Click "View Submissions" to see all uploads
  7. Review the color-coded plagiarism table
Understanding Plagiarism Results
Similarity % Status Meaning
0 – 30% Safe Original work, minimal overlap
30 – 60% Suspicious Some similarities found, needs review
60 – 100% High Plagiarism Significant copying detected
Frequently Asked Questions

Currently, we accept PDF (.pdf) and DOCX (.docx) files only. Make sure your file is within 10 MB. Other formats like .txt or .doc are not supported.

Plagiarism results are restricted to faculty only to maintain assessment integrity. Students can upload assignments but cannot view similarity scores or reports.

The similarity percentage is calculated using TF-IDF and cosine similarity. It measures how much text overlap exists between two submissions for the same assignment. A higher percentage indicates more shared content.

During registration, a 6-digit OTP is sent to your email. Enter it on the verification screen to complete your registration. The OTP expires after 10 minutes.
